A Message from Our Faculty

Welcome to the Boston Avengers Academy. You've displayed exceptional abilities and a desire to use them for the greater good. We offer specialized training to help you reach your full potential as a hero.

Our Program

The BAA provides comprehensive superhero education through a six-month residential program. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical experience, preparing you for the challenges of heroic service.

Our experienced instructors include active Avengers who share their expertise in combat, ethics, law enforcement cooperation, and team dynamics. You'll train in state-of-the-art facilities including our advanced Danger Room simulation chamber.

What We Offer

  • Professional hero training from active Avengers
  • Full room and board during training
  • Weekly living stipend
  • Medical and life insurance coverage
  • Access to advanced training facilities
  • Ongoing mentorship opportunities
  • Vehicle and equipment access
  • Potential for Avengers membership

Curriculum Highlights

Your training covers essential areas of modern heroics:

  • Power control and development
  • Combat and tactical training
  • Law and ethics
  • Investigation techniques
  • Team operations
  • Superhero history and culture

Student Life

You'll live on campus during the six-month program, training six days per week with Sundays free. Our facilities include:

  • Private dormitory rooms
  • Training grounds
  • Research library
  • Dining facilities
  • Recreation areas
  • Medical center

After Graduation

Successful graduates enter a three-month probationary period as junior heroes. You'll receive:

  • Official Avengers ID
  • Access to Avengers facilities
  • Ongoing mentorship
  • Weekly stipend
  • Equipment privileges
  • Advanced training opportunities

About Boston Avengers Academy

Our Mission

Boston Avengers Academy trains exceptional individuals to serve and protect while upholding strong ethical standards.

History & Organization

Founded with support from the Avengers and key benefactors, BAA operates as both an educational facility and a secure headquarters for its heroes.

Academy Facilities

  • Main Campus (West Street, Boston) – Training facilities, dormitory rooms, research labs, medical center, dining hall, and secure entrances (rear alley door, rooftop access, underground tunnel, and ID-secured elevator).
  • Meeting Room (2 Seaport Lane) – A 9th floor office with anti-eavesdropping protection, advanced threat detection, and meeting spaces.
  • North End Warehouse – Vehicle fleet storage with access to motorcycles, cars, boats, and a helicopter (special permission required), supported by 24/7 security.

Training Program

The Academy delivers a six-month program with six training days per week. Courses run in two-week modules with small classes to emphasize team-focused training. Faculty include primary instructors and guest lecturers.

  • Power control and development
  • Combat and tactical training
  • Law, ethics, and team operations
  • Investigation techniques and crisis response

Governance & Policies

The Academy follows strict policies to protect its operations and maintain secrecy.

  • BAA Charter – Outlines our mission and legal framework.
  • BAA Bylaws – Defines the rules and regulations that guide our daily operations.

Post-Graduation Benefits

After graduation, students enter a three-month probationary period as junior heroes and unlock a range of benefits:

  • Avengers ID Card – Features include:
    • ID verification
    • Audio/video communication
    • Global satellite uplink
    • GPS tracking
    • Computer access pad
    • Vehicle ignition key
    • Facility access key
    • Identity concealment mode
    • Waterproof and pressure-resistant design
  • Advanced Training – Graduates gain access to:
    • Optional seminars (with a 2-week notice)
    • Danger Room sessions
    • Specialized instruction
  • Additional Resources – Available benefits include:
    • Training facilities and research labs
    • Vehicle fleet and medical care
    • Housing options and mentorship
    • Technical support and a weekly stipend
  • Continued benefits include facility and vehicle access, academy housing options, and medical/life insurance.

Post-Graduation Requirements

All graduates must adhere to specific regulations to maintain the integrity and secrecy of the Academy:

  • Maintain their secret identity
  • Attend all scheduled training sessions
  • Follow ethical guidelines
  • Report all superhero activities
  • Protect academy secrecy
  • Cooperate with law enforcement
  • Follow the chain of command
